Privacy Policy of Info Docente

Last updated: [Insert date]

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Info Docente collects, uses, and protects personal data obtained from users.

1. Data Controller

Responsible: Fran Cazalla
Email: infodocente@gmail.com
Location: Spain

2. Data Collected

The app collects data provided by the user, data obtained from official portals of the Junta de Andalucía, data derived from usage, and data collected through advertising and analytics tools.

2.1 Data Provided During Registration

  • Email address
  • Password for the app
  • Credentials for official education portals (stored only on the user’s device)

2.2 Data Obtained From Portal Docente

Info Docente retrieves teaching positions, dates, center codes, names, provinces, and localities.

2.3 Data Obtained From the Junta Education Portal

The app retrieves service time, convocations, test results, baremo, order number, observations, preferences, inactivity status, and disability accreditation.

2.4 Public Data From SIPRI

The app retrieves public information such as center, province, job type, and more.

3. External Services

The app uses advertising and analytics tools compliant with EU GDPR.

4. Purpose of Processing

To display consolidated teacher information, manage service data, and improve user experience.

5. Legal Basis

Consent and performance of a requested service.

6. Security and Storage

Data is stored securely in servers located in Spain. Sensitive data is encrypted.

7. Data Sharing

Info Docente does not sell or share data except with service providers necessary for app functionality.

8. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, limitation, or portability of their data by emailing infodocente@gmail.com.

9. Account Deletion

Users can request deletion at any time. Server data is erased within 30 days.

10. Under-age Users

The app is only for adults. Minors under 16 must not use it.

11. Changes to This Policy

Users will be informed of significant changes.

12. Disclaimer

Info Docente is an independent application and is not affiliated with the Junta de Andalucía. It does not modify or submit information on behalf of the user. All data is informational and may not reflect real-time updates from official portals.
